I am a graduate from the UW-Madison database group and was very fortunately advised by Prof. Paris Koutris. I solve data management problems with principled guarantees and defended my thesis on some new results on consistent query answering (CQA). Before that, I worked with Prof. Yixin Cao at HK PolyU on graph problems. Currently, I am exploring opportunities at Meta.
